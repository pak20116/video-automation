
Artificial intelligence is rapidly transforming modern society. Technologies that seemed like science fiction just a few years ago are now seamlessly integrating into our daily lives. In the medical field, AI detects cancer early and drastically shortens the time required for new drug development. AI can process thousands of years of medical data, which would take thousands of specialists years to analyze, in just a few hours.A revolution is also taking place in education. AI tutors provide customized learning tailored to the pace and comprehension level of each individual student, opening an era where anyone, anywhere in the world, can receive top-tier education. However, these changes simultaneously bring new challenges. Social discussions regarding shifts in the job market, data privacy, and AI ethics have become more important than ever. Ultimately, the future of artificial intelligence does not depend on the technology itself, but rather on how we, as humans, utilize it. Wise choices are required of us at this very moment to build a better world with AI.
